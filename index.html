About

Creative and detail-oriented, designing residential, commercial, hospitality, retail and banking interiors — with a global perspective shaped across Oman, the UK and India.

Hritika holds a Master's degree in Interior Design for Health and Wellbeing from Buckinghamshire New University (UK) and a Bachelor's in Interior Design from the Scientific College of Design (Oman), bringing a design perspective enriched by diverse cultural influences.

Currently working as a Team Leader, she manages a quality-driven design studio — overseeing multiple projects from concept through execution, leading collaborative design processes, refining deliverables, developing studio templates and ensuring standards are consistently met across every phase.

She is experienced in translating client visions into functional, aesthetically considered spaces, with a strong commitment to sustainability and WELL standards, and is proficient in concept development, material selection, space planning and cross-disciplinary coordination.
